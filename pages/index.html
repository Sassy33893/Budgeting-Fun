Build-A-Budget

Making Finances Easy!




This is a budgetting tool that can help individuals track their income, expenses, debts and goals all in one place! The user can start by adding their current

financial information and continue adding it as time goes by. The user will be provided with pie charts and bar graphs to illustrate their financial standings.




By: Sasvin K., Shajeenth S., Ritvik G. and Jack L.
